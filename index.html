Health Hub

Be confident in your health.

During this pandemic, the decision to visit the doctor can be a difficult one. However, medical needs and conditions don't just go away.

Health Hub is your online doctor! It's a smart, simple, and secure tool that uses data science and machine learning to diagnose medical conditions.

Smart

Health Hub predicts your likelihood of suffering from life-altering health conditions. Our web application connects to IOT devices in your home to automatically gather data, which is processed by a machine learning model supported by data from thousands of patients.

Simple

Health Hub is simple to use.
Once you set up your account, our tool will track and analyze important health information to determine how healthy you are. Real-time data and results will be accessible from the Health Hub dashboard.

Secure

We understand that medical data needs to stay private. Therefore, we use tools like blockchain - the world's most secure database implementation - to ensure that your information is stored and used safely. Health Hub has you covered.
